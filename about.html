Our lodge has been through some ups and downs over the years, but we are hoping to pour our hearts into it and see it grow to host a number of games across various venues in the area. We currently host games at Nerd HQ in Johnson City, TN.



Get directions to Nerd HQ

We currently host games on Saturdays from 6:30PM-11:30PM EST. Sometimes our games run a little later, though we strive to prevent that from happening in order to respect everyone's time. If you are interested in signing up for a game, create an account on WarHorn.net and Register for games at our lodge in order to sign up. We can help you get started from there.



Need help building a character for the first time playing, or want to get some help from a GM before your game? Then post in our NETN Pathfinder Lodge facebook group or email us at netnpathfinder@gmail.com
